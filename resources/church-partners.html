Resources and Information for Pillar Partners

What is a Pillar Partner?

A local church is a partnering ministry with Pillar College, working toward the academic, spiritual and social transformation of its community. By agreeing and sharing the value of Christian higher education with Pillar College, a Pillar Partner is a stakeholder of New Jersey’s only accredited Bible college.

Why become a Church Partner?

Pillar Partners give and receive valuable benefits that enrich their church community and equip their members for more effective service. Pillar College provides educational resources and scholarships that stimulate spiritual maturity, academic growth, and moral influence. By partnering with Pillar College, a local church strengthens and helps fulfill the mission of educating, inspiring, and equipping the next generation for its call to ministry.
