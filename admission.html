Admission Process

Follow the steps below to complete your admission to World Theology University.

Step 1 — Download the Admission Form

Begin your admission process by downloading the official Admission Application Form. Please fill it out completely and clearly.

Go to Admission Form

Step 2 — Fill Out the Form

Complete the Admission Application Form with all requested information.

Step 3 — Upload Your Completed Form

Upload your completed Admission Application Form using the Google Form below.

Upload Completed Form (Google Form)

You will be redirected to Google Forms to submit your application.

Alternative Option: Send by Email

If you prefer, you can also send your completed form directly to our admissions email:

Send via Email: worlduniversitytheology@gmail.com

Don't forget to attach your completed PDF form to the email.

Important Notes

Ensure all information is accurate before submitting your form. Our admissions team will contact you within 3–5 business days.

Need Assistance?

If you need assistance, contact our admissions office at:

admissions@worlduniversitytheology.com
