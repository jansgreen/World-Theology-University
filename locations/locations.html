Non-Discriminatory Policy to Students

Subject to the Constitution of the United States and all applicable state and federal laws, Pillar College does not discriminate against applicants or students on the basis of race, color, and national or ethnic origin in its admissions or in the administration of its education policies, programs, or activities.

Newark Campus

60 Park Pl, Newark, NJ 07102

  • 973-803-5000
  • 973-556-1481
  • info@pillar.edu

Welcome to the Newark Campus of Pillar College, located at 60 Park Place right next to the New Jersey Performing Arts Center. Newark is a dynamic and vibrant city for students, with a wide range of culture, sports, and dining. options. From our prime location in the heart of the city, you have a clear view of the New York skyline. Our Newark Campus is easily accessible via public transportation.
