Academics

Educate. Inspire. Equip.

Pillar College is an institutionally accredited learning community dedicated to building tomorrow’s leaders today. The only accredited evangelical Christian college in New Jersey, Pillar College has offered a Christ-centered education for over 100 years. We offer traditional Associate’s, Bachelor’s, and Master’s Degrees, as well as, accelerated and nontraditional academic pathways for adult learners.

When you visit any of our locations — in Newark, Somerset, Paterson, Plainfield, or Jersey City — you’ll find that Pillar College is more than an address. It’s a community of supporters. As such, we offer a quality comprehensive education with the highest possible accreditation.

Our advantage: programs that offer flexibility for a variety of life and career needs.

Our academic programs are tailored to fit the specific needs of each student. It is our goal to meet you where you are as you pursue a brighter future, and to ultimately help you get there. We take pride in helping you to discover, clarify and to achieve God’s greater vision for your life.
